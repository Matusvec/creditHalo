Common questions

I barely have any money — is this even for me?

Especially for you. Credit Halo is built for students starting from zero. The community is full of people in the same boat.

How is this different from Reddit personal finance?

Reddit is anonymous chaos. Credit Halo has real tools, daily lessons, peer benchmarks, and verified students at real universities.

Will my financial info be shared?

Never. All community data is anonymized. Your finances are private unless you choose to share.

Can I actually build wealth in college?

Yes. Students who start at 20 have a 10-year head start. Compound interest doesn't care about your GPA.
